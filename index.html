Our Story

A journey through the rich heritage of pure vegetarian Indian cuisine

Welcome to Satvik Bhoj

Since our inception, we have been dedicated to serving authentic pure vegetarian Indian cuisine that honors both tradition and taste. Every dish is prepared with the finest organic ingredients, following age-old recipes passed down through generations.

Our commitment to Satvik cooking principles ensures that every meal nourishes not just the body, but also the mind and soul. We believe in the power of pure, wholesome food to transform lives and bring people together.

Experience the authentic flavors of India in an atmosphere of warmth, elegance, and tradition.

Chef's Specialties

Signature dishes that define our culinary excellence

Rajasthani Thali

A royal feast featuring 12 traditional Rajasthani dishes served with five types of breads, three types of rice, and traditional desserts. A complete journey through the flavors of Rajasthan.

South Indian Dosa Platter

An assortment of crispy dosas including Masala, Rava, and Paper Dosa served with authentic sambhar, coconut chutney, and special podi powder made from family recipes.

Punjabi Feast

Experience the richness of Punjab with our special thali featuring Dal Makhani, Shahi Paneer, Mixed Vegetable Korma, served with Butter Naan and aromatic Pulao.

Satvik Special Thali

Pure Satvik meal without onion and garlic, featuring seasonal vegetables, lentils, rice, breads, and sweets prepared following ancient Ayurvedic principles for complete nourishment.
